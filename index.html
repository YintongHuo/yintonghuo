About

I am a second year CSE Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. I received my Bachelor's degree in Digital Media Technology from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2020. My research focuses on Software Engineering and Knowledge Graph. Specifically, I spend my time and effort on following research topics:

  • AIOps: Identify, localize and analyze faults/anomalies in large cloud systems.
  • Knowledge Graph: Acquire structured information from natural language and conduct knowledge inference.
